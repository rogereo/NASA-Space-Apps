About Exoplanet Explorer

Exoplanet Explorer is a project dedicated to making the wonders of the universe accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide up-to-date information, research, and educational resources about exoplanets—planets orbiting stars beyond our solar system.

Whether you're a student, educator, researcher, or simply curious about the cosmos, Exoplanet Explorer offers tools and insights to help you discover new worlds and expand your understanding of the universe.
